Our Journey on Wheels

Connecting Villages and Cities through Taste+Technology+Travel

FoodWheels India🍔🚚

FoodWheels India - The Travelling Food Truck Website is not just a food project — it's a story, a brand, and a vision created by Manasa to unite people through taste, technology, and travel. What started as a small dream in 2025 with one truck has now grown into a travelling food revolution that connects villages and cities with the same plate of happiness.

From City people in Hyderabad to farmers in Machilipatnam, FoodWheels India ensures that everyone gets access to fresh, hygienic, and affordable food. “From City Streets to Village Roads — One Meal for All.”🌾🍛

👩‍🍳 About the Founder

Manasa

Founder & Visionary

Manasa started FoodWheels India with one goal — to bring delicious food closer to everyone, whether in cities or remote villages. My idea blends modern technology, social media, and street food culture to make food trucks a part of India's everyday journey.

“For me, food isn't just a meal — it's a emotion and a connection that travels with every wheel on the road.” ❤🚚
